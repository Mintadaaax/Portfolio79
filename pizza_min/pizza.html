Delicious Pizza Dip Recipe

Let's create a mouth-watering Pizza Dip!

Ingredients & Preparation

Recipe Instructions

Ingredients

FOR THE BREAD

  • 1 can biscuit dough (16.3 oz)
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 2 tsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

FOR THE DIP

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning, divided
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional: 8 slices pepperoni, quartered

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Cut each biscuit in half, roll into balls.
  • Mix butter, Parmesan, and 1 tsp seasoning.
  • Coat dough balls in butter mix, arrange around skillet.
  • Beat cream cheese, ricotta, and 2 tsp seasoning together.
  • Spread cheese mix in the skillet center, add marinara sauce and mozzarella.
  • Top with pepperoni, if desired. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
